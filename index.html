🦟 Fall Armyworm HIGH
Maize, Sorghum, Millet
Signs: Ragged holes, frass droplets, wilting leaves.
Action: Neem-based pesticide early morning. Report to MAAIF.
🍌 Banana Wilt (BXW) HIGH
Matoke, Banana
Signs: Yellowing/wilting leaves, oozing from flower.
Action: Remove & bury infected plants. Sterilize tools.
🦗 Cassava Mosaic MEDIUM
Cassava
Action: Use resistant varieties (NASE 14, NASE 19).
🐛 Coffee Berry Borer MEDIUM
Robusta & Arabica Coffee
Action: Collect & destroy fallen berries. Apply Beauveria bassiana.
🌿 Maize Streak Virus LOW
Maize
Action: Plant streak-resistant varieties. Control leafhoppers.

Groundnuts
Action: Use certified virus-free seed. Control aphid vectors.

Organic Farming

Farm without synthetic chemicals using natural compost, biological pest control and crop rotation.

Terrace Farming

Grow on hillside terraces to prevent erosion — ideal for Uganda's hilly Kigezi and Rwenzori regions.

Aquaponics

Combine fish farming and vegetables in a closed-loop system using 90% less water.

Vertical Farming

Grow crops in stacked layers indoors — perfect for urban Kampala, Jinja, and Entebbe.

Hydroponics

Soil-free growing with nutrient-rich water. Produces 3–10× more yield in the same space.

Integrated Farming

Combine crops, livestock and trees on one farm for maximum productivity all year round.

Climate-Smart Farming

Adapt to Uganda's changing rainfall using drought-tolerant seeds and water harvesting.

Agroforestry

Grow crops among trees for shade, soil health, and an extra income from timber or fruit.
